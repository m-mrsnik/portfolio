Case Studies

Selected work

LXE Hearing — Ecommerce

Redesigning checkout to unlock financing

Problem: Buy Now, Pay Later financing was buried in checkout, creating friction right at the point of decision and suppressing adoption.

Solution: Led a checkout flow redesign that surfaced financing options earlier and clarified terms at the moment users needed them most.

Outcome: BNPL adoption increased 16%, reducing a major barrier to purchase.

Eargo — Product Detail Pages

Testing PDPs to cut buying friction

Problem: Product detail pages left shoppers with unanswered questions, slowing the path to purchase.

Solution: Redesigned and A/B tested PDP layouts and content to resolve open questions earlier in the journey.

Outcome: Higher conversion rate alongside a shorter time-to-purchase.

Eargo & LXE Hearing — Design Systems

Building a cross-brand design language

Problem: Design was inconsistent across three brands' websites, apps, and print materials, slowing teams and fragmenting the experience.

Solution: Created the organization's first component-based design system, then evolved it into a shared, accessible language across web, mobile, and print.

Outcome: Consistent, WCAG 2.1 AA-compliant experiences across 3 brands, 3 websites, and 3 mobile apps.
